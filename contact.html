Request checklist

Ask customers for the information needed to give a helpful first response.

  • Name: Who should we contact?
  • Dog name and size: Helpful for planning the walk.
  • Neighborhood: Used to confirm service area.
  • Service needed: Walk, potty break, puppy visit, or check-in.
  • Preferred days/times: Makes scheduling easier.
  • Special notes: Leash habits, medication, gate codes, or temperament.
Builder note: For a real customer, this page could later be upgraded with Formspree, Tally, Google Forms, Calendly, or another booking tool.
